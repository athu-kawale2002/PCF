Live Life, King Size About Us!

Who We Are

We take pride in our unrivaled expertise in boxing and MMA relations, and event promotions. We believe that our hands-on experience and extensive knowledge of the sport are the pillars of what makes us great in the future.

Where We Are

We have firmly established our presence in Mumbai, India.

Our Mission

To represent and develop professional boxing and MMA in India. We are committed to providing a high level of professional support and services to our audience, promotors and events, career development, legal support, media consultation, and endorsement/sponsorship coordination. We also strive to present the highest level of sports entertainment through our events to our other important clients.

Our Vision

To continue to expand to new areas all over India as well as all over the world and assist as many MMA fighters and boxers as possible to reach their full potential both professionally and personally inside and outside of the cage.
